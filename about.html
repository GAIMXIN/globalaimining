About Global AI Mining

Bridging the Gap Between Academic Insight and Industrial Impact

At Global AI Mining, our mission is clear: to bridge the widening gap between cutting-edge academic research and real-world industrial execution in AI and machine learning. We are building an AI/ML ecosystem that empowers builders and researchers with the tools, infrastructure, and mentorship they need to launch real machine learning solutions — not just ideas.

We believe that artificial intelligence should be inclusive, actionable, and accessible. That's why we provide structured, hands-on learning experiences that enable individuals — from students to seasoned professionals — to upskill quickly and work on impactful AI/ML projects from day one.

Why We Exist

Two major shifts inspired us to act:

  • Disconnection between academia and industry: While academia continues to produce world-class research, industry adoption often lags behind. Many innovations remain trapped in papers and conferences — never making it to market.
  • The AI revolution is leaving many behind: As automation accelerates, millions face job displacement. Yet there's a shortage of over 150,000 data scientists in the U.S. alone. The need for accessible AI education and retraining has never been more urgent.

We aim to close both gaps — by training the next generation of AI-literate builders and helping traditional industries evolve with modern AI solutions.

Who We Are

Founded by Dr. Weiqing Gu, a McAlister Professor at Harvey Mudd College and former Program Director at the National Science Foundation (NSF), Global AI Mining brings together deep expertise in mathematics, machine learning, and education.

Dr. Gu has:

  • Published over 36 research papers in machine learning, geometry, and control theory.
  • Built anomaly detection systems for the U.S. Navy and Defense Threat Reduction Agency.
  • Presented her research to the U.S. Congress and was featured in Forbes for her innovative AI education programs.
  • Designed a new educational model where students learn ML and math like a game — intuitive, engaging, and effective.

Built for Builders

Our ecosystem is engineered for builders — engineers, scientists, creators, and learners who want to build the future. Whether you're a professional retraining for a new role or a student preparing for industry, we help you grow your skillset and execute AI solutions with confidence.
